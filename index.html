Projects & Case Studies

Velora – Fashion E-Commerce Mobile App

Designed a modern fashion shopping application focused on enhancing the complete customer journey, from product discovery to post-purchase support. The design emphasizes usability, accessibility, and a seamless user experience.

Problem: Many e-commerce apps provide limited support after a purchase. Users often struggle to track their orders, contact support when issues arise, and understand which actions require authentication. This creates frustration and reduces trust in the platform.

Solution: Users can track their orders in one place with clear and simple updates about delivery status. They can easily contact the seller or support with just one tap if any problem happens. The app only asks for login when necessary, so the experience stays smooth and easy.
